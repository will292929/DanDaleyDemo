Here is where I stand on issues:

Protect the Marine & Resource Economy

I will push back against federal and state overregulation that threatens our working water-front, Shellfishing, groundfishing, aquaculture, logging, and trades. Environmental policy must protect natural resources without destroying the jobs and traditions that sustain our communities.

Energy Affordability & Reliability

Heating oil, electricity, and transportation costs are crushing families and small businesses. I oppose policies that rapidly electrify without realistic infrastructure and cost analysis. We need affordable, reliable energy and not ideological experiments.

Taxes & Cost of Living Relief

In a low-wage, high-cost region, every new tax hits hard. I will oppose unnecessary tax increases and support measures that protect seniors, fixed-income residents, and working families from rising property and energy burdens.

Veterans & Law Enforcement Support

Support for veterans, shipyard workers, and law enforcement must be more than repetitive rhetoric. We need policies that strengthen defense-related employment and ensure public safety resources are properly funded.

Local Control Over Top-Down Mandates

Too many policies are imposed on towns with little regard for local impact. I support town-level decision-making on land use, waterfront access, and community development. Rural Maine should not be governed as an afterthought, by bureaucrats in Augusta.

Property Rights & Working Waterfronts

Protect private property rights and preserve commercial waterfront access for working families. Development and conservation policy must respect livelihoods, not eliminate them.

Second Amendment & Rural Culture

Hunting, firearms ownership, and self-reliance are part of Maine’s heritage. I support constitutional rights and oppose legislation that penalizes responsible gun owners.
